Where Did it Begin?

Okay so let me tell you! It all began when I was very little, probably over a year old. Let me give you some context first. My mom came from Colombia when she was very young and her family still lived there. Being that most of her family lived there, she would get home sick and began taking me to Colombia with her. I obviously developed a relationship with my family, but I also fell in love with learning about the culture and growing up with it. We would go at least every other year. As time passed by, she began taking me to different places. She took me to Spain since my aunt lives there. My mom sent me to spend a whole summer in Spain. She also took me to Mexico a few times. All of these experiences have left a great mark on me and allowed me to develop a curiosity for culture and life!
