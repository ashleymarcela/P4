My Favorite Travel Experience

Now, let me tell you about my favorite travel experience! It was the summer that my mother sent me to Spain. I was 13 years-old at the time. I did not want to go at the time because of the fact that I was so attached to my mom and I didn't want to be away from her for a whole two months. I was not excited to go at all. It was not my first time in Spain nor was it my first time staying with my aunt, it was just my first time experiencing something so big without my mom. When I got there, it was tons of fun! That was the most active summer I've ever had, even to this day. Almost everyday we were up and moving doing something. We went to different beaches, monuments, historical landmarks, concerts, restaurants, lakes, friends houses, hikes, etc. I got to visit and explore so many different parts of Spain, especially southern Spain, and I got to learn to really appreciate the culture. The beatiful beaches, the big mountains, the delicious food, the wonderful music, the traditional dances, the language, the dialect, everything there is just so wonderful.
